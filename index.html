    About Roleplay

    Roleplay is a type of activity where individuals assume and act out fictional roles, often in a structured or organized setting. It's like acting, but without a script. Participants create characters and interact with each other in a shared imaginary world. Roleplaying can take many different forms, from tabletop games like Dungeons and Dragons to online multiplayer games like World of Warcraft, to live-action roleplaying events. In recent years, the term "roleplay" has also been used to refer to a type of online interactive storytelling or improvisational writing, where participants create and write stories
