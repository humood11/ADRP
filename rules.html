Abu Dhabu Role-Play In-Game Rules

Rule 1 | Fail Roleplay (F-RP)

All Roleplays must be realistic and you must not do any actions that are deemed unrealistic.

Rule 2 | Vehicle Death Match (V-DM)

Do not hit/ram, anyone, with your car for no reason.

Rule 3 | Random Deathmatch (RDM)

You must not kill/harm another player without a valid reason to do so.

Rule 4 | Gun Motion

You must use gun motion whenever you pull out a weapon (except for pistols). An example of this, -Slings -, -GG-. Bringing out a taser or a pistol is exempt from this.

Rule 5 | Weapons

  • Remington 870
  • Skorpian
  • Remington MSR (sniper)
  • Vector SMG
  • Shotgun (cannot be used by any team).

Rule 6 | New Life Rule (NLR)

If the character you're using dies, you must roleplay as a new character without any previous knowledge of previous scenes.

Rule 7 | Cop Baiting

You must not purposefully commit a crime to grab an officer's attention.

Rule 8 | Cuff Rushing/Auto-arresting

You must say cuffs when arresting someone. You then need to transport the suspect to the county jail, you cannot auto-arrest them

Rule 9 | Safe Zone

Crimes are not allowed to be committed in the following areas: Police Station, Sheriff Station, Fire Station & DOT Station and Civilian Spawn.

Rule 10 | Prohibited Roleplays

Bomb, Suicide & Drug Roleplays are banned. Your roleplay must comply with Roblox's Community Standards.

Rule 11 | Interfering with scenes

If you were not originally involved with someone else's scene then do not attach yourself to it.

Rule 12 | Group Limits

Do not go around the map in large groups (maximum of 4). You must also have at least 2 people to rob the jewelry store and 4 people to rob the bank.
